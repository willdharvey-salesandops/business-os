## THE PROBLEM

**Section heading:** Sound familiar?

**Body:**

You built something real. A proper business, real clients, a team (or the start of one).

But somewhere along the way, you became the thing holding it all together. You're still closing most of the sales. Still the first call when something goes wrong. Still the one making decisions that probably don't need you.

The business runs because you do.

And while you're heads-down running it, everything else gets pushed: more time with family, staying active, actually thinking clearly.

You're not failing. You're just still in the middle of everything.

## WHAT CHANGES

**Section heading:** Here's what we actually work on

**Intro text:**
Not theory. Not generic goals. Specific things that free up your time and move the business forward without you having to push everything yourself.

**Outcome cards:**

### Hire properly
Build the role, find the right person, onboard them so they can actually do the job. Not just "hire someone and hope for the best."

### Get problems off your desk
Stop being the first call when something goes wrong. Build the structure, document the decisions, and trust the team to handle what shouldn't need you.

### Embed systems that actually save time
AI, automation, and process design. Not added noise, but real hours back. Applied to the parts of your business where you're doing things you shouldn't be.

### Know where your time should go
Work out what only you can do, and stop doing everything else. Clearer priorities, better decisions, less firefighting.

### Step back without things going quiet
Build a business that doesn't need you for everything. Then actually step back: family, sport, thinking time, without the guilt or the anxiety that it'll fall apart.

## HOW IT WORKS

**Section heading:** How we work together

**Step 1: Understand the real situation**
We start with a discovery call to understand where you are, what's actually getting in the way, and whether working together makes sense. If it does, we go deeper in a strategy session.

**Step 2: Build what's missing**
We get into the business together. I'll help you build the sales system, the hiring process, the team structure, the AI workflows, whatever it is that's keeping you stuck in the middle.

**Step 3: You step back (properly)**
With the right structure in place, you stop being the bottleneck. You spend more time on what actually grows the business, and more time on the rest of your life.

**Below the steps:**

> This isn't a course or a coaching programme you do alongside running your business. It's proper work, done together, inside your actual business.

## CLIENT STORY

**Section heading:** What it looks like in practice

I started working with one client back in 2020, just before Covid hit. He was a one-man band: a desk in a coworking space above a library, revenue around £100k, everything running through him.

Six years on, he's at £3 million. He has a team. He takes the school holidays off with his kids. He pays his people well and goes home in time for what matters.

He built that. But I was alongside him every step of the way. Through the uncertainty, the hard decisions, the moments it felt like a lot. We built his team structure together, wrote job descriptions, set up proper onboarding, and worked out what he should keep doing versus what he should hand off. We put the systems in place that took the weight off.

His week now splits between work and sport, a bit like a school timetable. He's still growing, still on the front foot, still building new service lines. But he does it from a position of choice, not obligation. He spends his time on what he's genuinely best at: the thinking, the vision, the ideas. And he's around for the things that matter outside of it.
